Web APIs

In this week, I have tried working around with different Web APIs. The experiments are as follows:

  • Movie API(1): In this, I made a pre-defined database of movie lists where we can add new movie or delete existing one.
  • Movie API(2) : In this, I am extracting data from IMDB API.
  • Movie API(3): Here I added 'Add to favorites' and 'Details' functionality.
  • Weather API: In this experiment, I am extracting weather information and displaying it.
